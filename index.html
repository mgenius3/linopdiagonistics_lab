    About Us

    LINO P Medical Diagnostics and Research Center is one of the leading indigenous medical diagnostic centres in Nigeria. Established in 1998 and with our key Diagnostics Complex situated in Festac Town, Lagos, Nigeria, we offer state-of-the-art medical diagnostic services to patients in Lagos and environs. The Center is one of the most comprehensive diagnostic facilities in Lagos and meets international standards. Our years of excellent service delivery and compliance to international procedures and standards, has earned us the ISO 15189:2012 Accreditation, one of only three laboratories in the entire country accredited to this global standard. The LINO P facility is also involved in medical research.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading and trusted medical diagnostic and research institution in Nigeria with consistently acceptable international standards

    Our Mission

    To be an indigenous standard and modern medical diagnostic institution that consistently makes steady scientific improvement in health care delivery combined with a culture of care and consistency. …consistently reliable

    The LINOP Medical Diagnostics and Research Center is fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and the Medical Laboratory Science Council of Nigeria (MLSCN); accredited by the National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) and the Lagos State Health Facility Monitoring and Accreditation Agency (HEFAMAA) as a secondary healthcare provider. We are recipients of several Merit Awards of Achievement and are in partnership with several agencies as part of support to the healthcare delivery system.

    Clinical Chemistry

    Specializing in biochemical analysis of body fluids to assist in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases.

    Our Clinical Chemistry department performs a wide range of tests, including blood glucose, liver function tests, lipid profiles, and more. With state-of-the-art equipment and certified professionals, we ensure accurate and timely results.

    Hematology

    Focusing on the study of blood and blood-forming tissues to diagnose blood-related disorders.

    Our Hematology department offers a full range of tests including complete blood counts (CBC), coagulation profiles, and other specialized tests to assess blood health and diagnose conditions like anemia, clotting disorders, and leukemia.

    Microbiology

    Providing diagnostic testing for infectious diseases by identifying bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens.

    Our Microbiology department specializes in the culture and identification of microorganisms, antimicrobial susceptibility testing, and infection control. We support healthcare providers in diagnosing and managing infectious diseases efficiently.

    Immunology

    Specializing in testing related to the immune system and autoimmune diseases.

    Our Immunology department performs a variety of tests to assess immune function and diagnose conditions such as allergies, autoimmune disorders, and immunodeficiencies. Our advanced assays provide reliable and accurate results.

    Pathology

    Providing detailed analysis of tissue samples to diagnose diseases.

    Our Pathology department offers services in histopathology and cytopathology, providing crucial information for the diagnosis and treatment of diseases, including cancer. Our pathologists work closely with clinicians to ensure comprehensive diagnostic support.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions regarding our laboratory services and practices

    What safety protocols are followed in the laboratory?

    We adhere to strict safety guidelines including the use of personal protective equipment (PPE), regular sanitization of work areas, and proper disposal of hazardous materials. Our lab staff undergo regular safety training to ensure compliance with regulatory standards.

    How do you ensure the accuracy of test results?

    Our laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and follows standardized protocols for all tests. Additionally, our quality control team performs regular checks and validations to maintain high-precision outcomes in every test conducted.

    What types of samples can be submitted for testing?

    We accept a variety of sample types including blood, urine, tissue, and environmental swabs, depending on the type of testing required. Please consult our sample submission guidelines for specific instructions related to your test.

    How long does it take to receive test results?

    The turnaround time for test results varies depending on the complexity of the test. Routine tests are typically processed within 24-48 hours, while specialized tests may take up to a week. We ensure prompt communication of results as soon as they are available.

    What certifications does your laboratory hold?

    Our laboratory is accredited by internationally recognized bodies such as ISO and CAP (College of American Pathologists). We continuously strive to meet and exceed the rigorous standards set by these organizations.

    How can I schedule a laboratory test?

    You can schedule a test by contacting our customer service or through our online booking system. Once your appointment is confirmed, you will receive instructions on how to prepare for the test and what to expect during your visit.
